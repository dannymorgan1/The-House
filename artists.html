Chris Stussy

Chris Stussy, whose real name is Niels Christian Steenbergen, is a Dutch house and electronic music artist known for his groovy bass lines and jazzy melodies. Born in Leidrop, Netherlands in 1994, Stussy began his career by playing at local clubs and has since gained recognition among house fans worldwide. His number one hit, All Night Long, became an overnight sensation when it was released in 2023 and has since racked up over 18 million streams on Spotify. Mall Grab

Known for his gritty, lo-fi and grime-inspired sound, Mall Grab has stood out as a leader of the modern house genre. He began his career by producing grime tracks and later transitioned to a house sound, but the influence of his early days can still be felt in his recent releases. I've Always Liked Grime is one of his most beloved tracks and also a nod to his early career. Kettama

Evan Campbell, better known as Kettama, is a Galway native making waves in the techno music industry. His raw, old-school and high-energy techno tracks have catapulted the Irishman to fame. Kettama's unique style can be attributed to the fact that he produces his tracks exclusively "in the box", meaning he doesn't use external hardware for his music productions. His style is 90's-esque and is influenced heavily by techno legends, such as Palace and Glenn Underground. His top single, B.O.D.Y., is popular among house enthusiasts and was famously played by house icon Mall Grab. Listen to a sample from the song that put Kettama on the map, Eastside Avenue.
